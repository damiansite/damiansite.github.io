Competitions

Below are presented the competitons in which I participated and explained for people who are not familiar with them. You can see the results I achieved in profile.

Olympiads

  • School Phase
    • This phase is organised by the school, from where only some of them are promoted to next phase. The number of people per class allowed to pass depends on the number of parallel classes (for example 11 "A", 11 "B" and 11 "C" would mean that only 3 people can go from 11-th class). The difficulty is varied depending on the teacher's selection of problems.
  • District Phase
    • This phase is organised by city administration. This phase is present only in cpaital, Chișinău due to the big number of participants. It's purpose is to filter and from around 100 students to get to best 25-30 students. The difficulty also is dependant on creators, but usally is an above school level, with more advanced problems.
  • Municipal/Regional Phase
    • This phase is still organised by the city administration, but the problems are made similar for the whole republic. From 30 students in the capital are chosen only 6 which pass to next phase, and in the republic's regions only 1st passes. The difficulty is bigger sometimes present problems from previous old international competitions or other sources.
  • Republican Phase
    • This phase is uniting the whole republic and from all the students who passed only 6 achieve a place and 4 mentions per class. From each class these 10 people are able to participate for Olympic Slot Selection. The difficulty is harder compared to previous phases.
  • Olympic Slot Selection Phase
    • From the selectd people only 4-5 are going to pass to the Olympic Slot which participates internationally.

If you achieve a place, you come back at same phase following year without participating previous years. Also only Mathematics and Romanian are from 7-th class. The rest of the subjects start from 9-th class.

"Mihai Marinciuc" Memorial Physics Competition

Organised by Technical University of Moldova, where students from the whole republic can participate. Problems (romanian and russian only) and results can be found on their site: fizica.utm.md

"Constantin Spătaru" Memorial Mathematics Competition

It was disbanded thru the years, but previously when it still took place it was an equivalent of Municipal Olympiad, even if there participated students from schools out of the city. Additionally compared to the official olympiads, this competition was from 4-th class/
