Competitions

Below are presented the competitons in which I participated and explained for people who are not familiar with them. You can see the results I achieved in profile.

Olympiads

If one person takes 1st, 2nd or 3rd place he shouldn't pass the previous phases and only comes back to the phase he took place last year. Otherwise if you have no prior participations, you start from start. Additionally most subjects start from 9th class, with some exceptions: Romanian and Mathematics (from 7th class), Informatics (from 8th class).

  • School Phase
    • Organised in each lyceum separately and are different, has the purpose of selecting from each school the best of the best and send them to represent the lyceum at further phases. Difficulty varies from teacher to teacher.
  • District Phase
    • Organised only in Chișinău and has as purpose to filter the best 25-30 out of all students. Those 25-30 participate afterwards at next Phase. The test should have a difficulty a little higher than school.
  • Municipal/Regional Phase
    • From Chișinău pass first 6 students, from other big cities might pass 3 and from other regions from the republic only 1st. The test shows requires a little more advanced level compared to previous phase.
  • Republican Phase
    • First 10 from each class (6 places and 4 mentions) pass to next phase (if they want to participate). The difficulty is the highest mostly unique and new problem sets for the whole republic.
  • Olympic Slot Selection Phase
    • From the participating people only 4-5 are going to pass to the Olympic Slot which participates internationally.

"Mihai Marinciuc" Memorial Physics Competition

Organised by Technical University of Moldova, where students from the whole republic can participate. Problems (romanian and russian only) and results can be found on their site: fizica.utm.md

"Constantin Spătaru" Memorial Mathematics Competition

It was disbanded thru the years, but previously when it still took place it was an equivalent of Municipal Olympiad, even if there participated students from schools out of the city. Additionally compared to the official olympiads, this competition was from 4-th class/
