Primary Education

I did my first years of school here, where I first found my passion towards mathematics. Here also is where I first time felt the joy participating competitions. Here I have put my base in every fundamental academic subject, which afterwards was developed.

Public Institution Theoretical Lyceum „Ștefan Cel Mare”

Lower Secondary Education

During Secondary Education I understood the meaning and the power of participating and taking places at competitions. At this lyceum I further advanced my mathematics and as well places fundamental basis in physics, chemistry and informatics which resulted to be very useful in my general development.

Public Institution Theoretical Lyceum „Ion Creangă”

Higher Secondary Education

This lyceum was previously known as Lyceum of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova, which lately changed under the supervision of Ministry of Education, and became P.I.R.T.L. „Aristotel”. This felt as one of the biggest achievements to get here, where the target is not to learn everything but to become proficient in what you love the most. This given me the chance to focus more on physics, informatics and mathematics rather than history, geography or biology. As my journey here is not finished yet, there is much more to come.

Public Institution Republican Theoretical Lyceum „Aristotel”
