"Mihai Marinciuc" Memorial Physics Competition

Organised by Technical University of Moldova, where students from the whole republic can participate. Problems (romanian and russian only) and results can be found on their site: fizica.utm.md

"Constantin Spătaru" Memorial Mathematics Competition

It was disbanded thru the years, but previously when it still took place it was an equivalent of Municipal Olympiad, even if there participated students from schools out of the city. Additionally compared to the official olympiads, this competition was from 4-th class/
